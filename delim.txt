System Delimitations

- Covers internal dealership operations only: users, inventory, allocations, preparation, test drives, reports, profile, settings.
- Focuses on Isuzu vehicle stock monitoring and movement inside the organization.
- Supports role-based access for admin, supervisor, manager, sales agent, dispatch, and driver.
- Handles unit allocation to agents and driver assignment/tracking for delivery flow.
- Manages vehicle preparation requests up to ready-for-release and release history.
- Handles test drive scheduling, approval, completion, cancellation, and no-show monitoring.
- Includes in-app operational notifications, with planned support for email/SMS/push.
- Generates reports for vehicles, allocations, audit trail, and release history.

Out of Scope / Boundaries
- No direct payment, billing, accounting, or invoicing module.
- No customer self-service portal; actions are mainly staff-operated.
- No full ERP, CRM, or supplier management coverage.
- No external government registration, insurance, or financing processing shown.
- No real production backend/API integration is confirmed yet; many flows use local/mock data.
- No guaranteed real-time GPS/maps integration yet; tracking UI appears partial/mock.
- No confirmed production email/SMS gateway implementation yet.
- No warehouse, spare parts, or service workshop management module.
- No advanced analytics/forecasting/BI beyond listed reports.
